If You Feel Unsafe

  1. Trust your instincts and leave immediately
  2. Go to a public area with other people
  3. Call campus police or 911 if needed
  4. Report the incident to BoilerSwap
  5. Consider using SafeWalk for future meetings

If Someone Doesn't Show Up

  1. Wait in a safe location for a reasonable time
  2. Try contacting them once more
  3. Don't wait alone in isolated areas
  4. Report repeated no-shows to us
  5. Consider the item available again

Report Safety Concerns

If you experience any safety issues or inappropriate behavior, please report it immediately.

üöÄ Getting Started

How do I create an account?

You need a valid @purdue.edu email address to sign up. Click "Sign In" in the header, then "Sign Up" and follow the verification process.

Is BoilerSwap really free?

Yes! BoilerSwap is completely free to use. All items shared on the platform are given away for free by fellow students.

Who can use BoilerSwap?

BoilerSwap is exclusively for Purdue University students, faculty, and staff with valid @purdue.edu email addresses.

How do I verify my Purdue email?

After signing up, we'll send a verification link to your @purdue.edu email. Click the link to activate your account.

üìù Posting Items

How do I post an item?

Click the "Post Item" button, fill out the form with item details, photos, and pickup information. Your item will be live immediately!

Can I edit my post after submitting?

Currently, you'll need to delete and repost your item to make changes. We're working on an edit feature!

What items can I post?

Most items are allowed except weapons, illegal substances, live animals, and hazardous materials. Check our terms for the full list.

How long do posts stay active?

Posts expire based on urgency: Urgent (24 hours), Soon (3 days), Flexible (7 days). You can mark items as picked up anytime.

Should I include my phone number?

You can, but we recommend using your Purdue email for initial contact. Only share your phone number with people you plan to meet.

Is BoilerSwap safe?

BoilerSwap is designed for the Purdue community, but you should always follow safety guidelines when meeting strangers.

Where should I meet for pickups?

Always meet in public, well-lit areas. Campus locations like residence hall lobbies or the Union are ideal.

What if someone doesn't show up?

Contact us to report no-shows. Repeated offenders may have their accounts restricted. Always have backup plans.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

Use our "Report Issue" page to report any safety concerns, harassment, or inappropriate behavior immediately.

Can I bring a friend to pickups?

Bringing a friend is encouraged, especially for evening pickups or when meeting someone new.
